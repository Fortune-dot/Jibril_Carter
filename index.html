BORN 1992 is a Tanzanian Norwegian graphic designer, photographer and DJ. An extraordinary photographer and graphic designer whose work is a captivating blend of contemporary expression and a deep reverence for moments and eras in pop and social culture. With a keen eye for detail, he skillfully captures the essence of seemingly bygone decades with a modern twist, proving that human expression never gets lost but that it rather branches.
